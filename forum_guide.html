My Personal Guide to the Forum

1st Step

To register: Click on Register and click on the box below that reads “I agree to these terms” Put in your information by following the prompts and submit. When all is done, the box in front of you would read thus: “Your account has been created. However, this board requires account activation; an activation key has been sent to the e-mail address you provided. Please check your e-mail for further information.” Close down the Forum Page completely.

2nd Step

Open your e-mail (the one with which you register on Forum). On the welcoming mail sent to you from Forum, click on the Link that takes you back to Forum. Now, login normally with the information given when you registered. Click on which of the Forum you intend working on. Type-in the Password given to you for that Forum and login to it. (You can only participate on the Forum to which you are invited, with the exception of the CONGREGATIONAL FORUM that is open to all). Then click on the Title of the Forum (You may choose to print out the Assignment and work on it separately. You may also choose to prepare your responses separately as to copy and paste it into the response page later (3rd step), or type it directly into the response page).

3rd Step

To send your responses in the way that other participants would be able to see it and write comment on it, repeat the 2nd step until you get to the Assignment. Click on “New Topic” at the left side of the Screen. Write your own title in the long Subject box (It could simply be “David’s Responses to …….” or anything) and then click on Submit.

4th Step

To comment on other participants responses, you would repeat the 2nd step until you see the Assignment and the responses of other. Read and pick the particular one you want to respond to. Click on it and then click on Reply below. You could type it directly into the reply page since it is not expected to be lengthy. There other possibilities such as: editing your responses; deleting your responses; attaching other documents to your responses. If you do not have anything to do with these other options, then click on Submit.
