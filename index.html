2012 Priority Message


It's incredible to realize that what we do each day has meaning in the big picture of God's plan - the one supreme business of life is to find God's plan for your life and live it.




"You are a chosen people. You are a kingdom of priests, God's holy nation, His very own possession. This is so you can show others the goodness of God, for He called you out of the darkness into His wonderful light." - 1 Peter 2:9 NLT



Rev. Tayo Martins has an apostolical call of God upon his life to prepare and equip Christian leaders desiring to move out of the position of spiritual weakness and timidity and into a position of strength, authority and boldness; to become violent in battle, and through prayers, turn to flight the armies of spiritual aliens in the Body of Christ (Hebrews 11:34). To bring about change so that God can move through the Prophetic Word, and that the people of God can move by the power of the Holy Spirit from captivity to freedom, lack to abundance, failure to success, stagnation to progress, hatred to love, rejection to acceptance, and barrenness to fruitfulness in the name of our Lord Jesus Christ.
