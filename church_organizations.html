Church Based Organizations
(Men, Women and Youth)

The following are guidelines provided to instruct the group on how to discharge its functions and duties within the local church; how to discover through worship, study and service, the will of God for themselves, for the church, and for all humanity. It provides directions on how to coordinate the total programs of the group, and help members to take active participation in the life of the church and mission of this Ministry.

1.) They are directly under the supervision of the Presiding Pastors.

2.) They are to meet together as many times as necessary to advice and counsel one another.

3.) They are to forward the Organization’s Annual Plans and Programs of Events to the Presiding Pastors for consideration and approval, not later than November of every year.

4.) They are to commit themselves to prayer and sharing the word of God regularly.

5.) They are to interact and maintain good relationship with one another as members of God’s family.

6.) They are to help each other in time of need and to exchange information regarding Job/Trade, Accommodation and other opportunities.

7.) They are to avoid sectionalism, favoritism, politics and other vices that could adversely affect the spiritual life of their members, the Church and/or the Vision of the Ministry.

8.) They are to support and co-operate with the Church authority in realizing and achieving the basic goals of the Ministry, and they must commit themselves to Literacy Training (e.g. LDP).

9.) They are to collaborate with other Functional Bodies in the Church to maintain a standard sanitation of the Hall of Worship, and to give it a befitting decoration at all times.
