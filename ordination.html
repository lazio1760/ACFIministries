Ordination and Licensing

The following are the conditions and qualifications of the ordination of the various categories of Ministers (Deacon/ness; Elder; Pastor) in this Ministry.

(a) Must be a full member and studied (stayed) with the Ministry to a reasonable level.

(b) Must agree with the fundamental doctrines of the Ministry and living a life that is worthy of emulation.

(c) Must have understood, accepted and willing to work with the Vision of the Ministry.

(d) If married, all the three levels of marriage rites must have been performed: customary, judiciary and Holy matrimony (or Marriage blessing as the case may be).

(e) May have the call of God.
